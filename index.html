My name is Christiana Ondrak, aka Wild Raccoon and I really enjoy creating all kinds of stuff.
I started with traditional painting first but quickly I got interested in digital art and began working with Photoshop, Illustrator and Procreate.
Surrealism, space and animals are the main topics I am using in my pictures. Looking at my artworks you will notice that I also have a little obsession with raccoons, that's also the reason for my artist name.
I was thinking about selling my art so I opened my own online shop at Redbubble and Displate.
Recently I began to create paintings with Midjourney AI which is a funny tool to experiment with.
As I always enjoy learning new skills I am currently into programming with HTML and CSS. This is how I got the idea of creating my own website.
So yeah, basically this is the whole story so have fun exploring it and I really hope my art will bring you joy!
